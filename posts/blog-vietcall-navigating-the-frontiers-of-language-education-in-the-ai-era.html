VietCALL: Navigating the Frontiers of Language Education in the AI Era

📅 1 tháng 1, 2024January 1, 2024 ✍️ VietCALL Uncategorized
← Quay lại Tin tứcBack to News
📰

Technology has become a defining force in contemporary education, and nowhere is its influence more consequential than in language teaching and learning. The integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) with established pedagogical practice is reshaping how languages are acquired, assessed, and taught. VietCALL — the Vietnamese Association of Computer-Assisted Language Learning — is positioned at this intersection, committed to advancing the field through research, collaboration, and professional development.

VietCALL brings together educators, researchers, and practitioners in Vietnam and internationally who seek to understand, critically evaluate, and leverage the potential of AI in language education. The association provides a platform for substantive scholarly exchange, where those shaping the future of language learning can engage with current research and emerging practice.

The Evolution of Language Teaching

Language teaching has undergone sustained methodological development, progressively moving beyond transmission-based models towards learner-centred and technology-enhanced approaches. VietCALL contributes to this evolution by examining how AI can be applied purposefully to language instruction — maintaining fidelity to the principles of effective pedagogy while exploring the possibilities that emerging tools offer.

Conversational AI tools such as ChatGPT represent a significant development in the landscape of language learning. Their capacity to engage learners in dynamic, contextually responsive dialogue provides new opportunities for communicative practice, immediate feedback, and autonomous learning. VietCALL explores these tools critically, considering both their affordances and their limitations within Vietnamese educational contexts.

The Collaborative Community of VietCALL

VietCALL operates as a collaborative scholarly community. Through its conferences, webinars, and online forums, the association brings together professionals from diverse institutional and disciplinary backgrounds to discuss the challenges and possibilities of integrating AI tools into language education. Members share pedagogical approaches, engage with current research, and contribute to a collective understanding of best practices in the field.

Research, Innovation, and Best Practice

A core commitment of VietCALL is the dissemination of current research in AI and language education. The association provides researchers, academics, and practitioners with opportunities to present their findings, contributing to a shared understanding of how technology can meaningfully enhance language teaching and learning.

VietCALL also curates resources on effective practice, drawing on the experiences of educators who have successfully integrated AI tools into their classrooms. These resources address contexts ranging from personalised learning and adaptive assessment to collaborative online environments, providing practical guidance for educators navigating the evolving terrain of AI-enhanced language instruction.

Join VietCALL

VietCALL invites educators, researchers, and language education professionals to join the association and contribute to the development of CALL in Vietnam. Members participate in an active scholarly community engaged in advancing both the theory and practice of technology-enhanced language education.

Through collective engagement with tools such as generative AI, VietCALL works towards a future in which language education is informed by rigorous research, responsive to learner needs, and enriched by the responsible integration of technology. We welcome your participation.
